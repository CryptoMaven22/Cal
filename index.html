What Cal proves

Clarity before dispatch.

Built around the L.E. Isley & Sons Plumbing workflow, Cal demonstrates a reusable AtlasFlow pattern for field service: a messy request becomes AI triage, a structured job record, Operational Viewer visibility, human review, and a clear recommended next action.

Product thesis

Service requests arrive messy and inconsistent, forcing dispatchers to interpret urgency, risk, and next steps manually. Cal can turn that unstructured input into clear, prioritized, dispatch-ready work while directing owner attention to the requests that matter most.

Validation question

Do Cal’s core artifacts help L.E. Isley & Sons Plumbing turn messy service requests into faster, more consistent decisions by surfacing urgent or high-risk work sooner and clarifying the right next action—without removing human control?
